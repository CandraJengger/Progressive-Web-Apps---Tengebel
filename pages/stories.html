Bantarangin Kingdom

Bantarangin Kingdom is a kingdom that was once located in the Ponorogo area. The story of the Bantarangin Kingdom became the basis for the Reog Ponorogo storyline. It is told of a king named Raja Klono Sewandhono who fell in love with Putri Kediri named Dewi Songgolangit. Long story short King Klono Sewandhono sent his governor named Pujonggo Anom to propose to Dewi Songgolangit in Kediri for him. When applying for Princess Songgoloangit it turned out that Princess Songgolangit provided several requirements that had to be fulfilled by Raja Klono Sewandhono. And Pujonggo Anom returned to Bantarangin Kingdom and told the conditions given by Princess Songgolangit to Raja Klono Sewandhono. Long story short, all the requirements are ready, only 1 requirement has not been obtained, namely a two-headed animal. Then he Raja Klono Sewandhono decided to go to Kediri to marry Dewi Songgonlangit by bringing a very busy procession, when on a trip when he wanted to enter the City of Kediri, a group of King Klono Sewandhono was attended by a Duke of Kediri who apparently also loved Dewi Songgolangit, he was Singo Barong. There was an extraordinary battle between Klono Sewandhono and Singo Barong, Klono Sewandhono was overwhelmed with Singo Barong because Singo Barong was able to turn into a Lion because it had the Gembong Tiger Ajian. Singo Barong lost and his head fused with a peacock so he became a lion-headed and peacock-headed human. And this fulfills the last requirement to apply for Dewi Songgolangit.

Wengker Kingdom

Wengker Kingdom is a Kingdom that existed in Ponorogo long before Bantarangin Kingdom. The Kingdom of Wengker was one time with the Kingdom of Kahuripan which was led by King Airlangga.

Jaka Lancur and Siti Amirah

Jaka Lancur and Siti Amirah are Ponorogo Remeo and Juliet. Where the love story ends tragically with death. This love story has become a popular story in Ponorogo. And become a hereditary story that must be told to the children and grandchildren in the villages of Mirah and Golan. Golan Village is the village of origin of Jaka Lancur and Mirah Village is the place of origin of Siti Amirah, there is a taboo in both villages, namely people from Golan Village may not be married to Orangan from Mirah Village and vice versa, because it will result wretch. This is the result of an oath from Jaka Lancur's father, Ki Honggolono

Bathoro Kathong

Bathoro Kathong was the first Regent of Ponorogo, he was one of the people who opened the Ponorogo area. His story is very famous for defeating Demang Ki Ageng Kuthu. When defeating Demang Ki Ageng Kuthu, he was not alone but was assisted by 2 friends, Ki Ageng Selo, and Kyai Ageng Amirah.

Baruklinthing

Baruklinthing is a story that comes from Ngebel, Ponorogo. The Baruklinthing story is a legend The origin of the Ngebel Lake. This story is believed by the Ngebel people, so that every month Suro there will be a ritual called Larung Sesaji.

Suminten Edan

Suminten Edan. It is a story of a woman who fell in love with a young man named Raden Mas Broto until he went crazy. This story is very popular in the Ponorogo area, until this story is often sung on the Ludruk stage which was often performed at Aloon aloon Ponorogo
